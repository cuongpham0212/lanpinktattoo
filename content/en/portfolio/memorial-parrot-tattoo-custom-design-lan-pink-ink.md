## Memorial Parrot Tattoo – A Real Customer Story from Lan Pink Ink

Every tattoo carries its own story. This piece began with a male international customer who was travelling in Vietnam and wanted a meaningful tattoo to honor someone very special to him.

That companion was Gonzo, a Green Cheek Conure who had shared many memories with him. After Gonzo passed away, the customer wanted a tattoo on his wrist so he could see it every day, keeping the memory and emotional connection close to him.

After finding Lan Pink Ink, he sent a real photo of Gonzo and shared the story behind the tattoo idea.

*Gonzo’s memorial parrot tattoo was custom designed from the customer’s real photo at Lan Pink Ink.*

From that story, Lan Pink Ink began creating a custom tattoo design focused on capturing Gonzo’s expression, colors, feather details and unique character.

This piece is a clear example of Custom Tattoo Design at Lan Pink Ink, where each tattoo is created from the customer’s personal story, idea and meaning instead of using a ready-made design.
