## Gonzo Memorial Parrot Tattoo – A Real Story From a Lan Pink Ink Client

Every tattoo carries its own story. For this piece, the story began with a male international client who was traveling in Vietnam and wanted to create a meaningful tattoo in memory of his special companion.

That companion was Gonzo, a Green Cheek Conure who had shared many meaningful moments with him. After Gonzo passed away, the client wanted a wrist tattoo that he could see every day, as a way to keep those memories and that special bond close.

After finding Lan Pink Ink, the client sent real photos of Gonzo and shared his idea for a personal custom tattoo design.
