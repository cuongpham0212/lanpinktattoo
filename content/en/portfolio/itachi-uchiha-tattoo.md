## The idea behind this Itachi Uchiha tattoo

The client wanted a tattoo that clearly showed the spirit of Itachi Uchiha while still fitting naturally on the upper arm and matching the body shape.

The design was built around several key elements:

- A portrait of Itachi Uchiha.
- Red Sharingan as the main highlight.
- Crows associated with the character.
- Dark shading for depth.
- Red-orange effects to make the tattoo stand out.

The composition was arranged as a full upper arm piece, giving enough space for detail without making the design feel crowded.
