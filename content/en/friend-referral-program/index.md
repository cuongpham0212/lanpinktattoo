# Friend Referral Rewards Program

At **Lan Pink Tattoo**, loyal clients are more than customers. They are people who trust our studio enough to recommend it to friends, family members, and others who share a passion for tattoos.

To recognize that support, **Lan Pink Member** offers a **Friend Referral Rewards Program** for members.

This program allows members to earn additional reward points when introducing new clients to Lan Pink Tattoo or when accompanying referred friends on future visits.

---

## 1. Why Does This Program Exist?

At Lan Pink Tattoo, we understand that a sincere recommendation from a satisfied client is incredibly valuable.

When someone trusts your recommendation for a tattoo studio, it represents more than a new booking. It reflects trust, connection, and the sharing of a positive experience.

For that reason, referral rewards are designed to be more attractive than standard purchase-based point accumulation.

In simple terms:

* Clients earn points from their own purchases.
* Members receive extra rewards for referring new clients.
* The more actively members recommend and accompany friends, the more points they can earn.

---

## 2. Who Can Participate?

This program is available to **Lan Pink Member** members whose information is registered in the Lan Pink Tattoo system.

Members may refer:

* Friends
* Family members
* Coworkers
* Acquaintances interested in getting tattooed
* Previous Lan Pink Tattoo clients who return together with the member

---

## 3. Rewards for Referring a New Client

When a member refers a new client to Lan Pink Tattoo, referral points are awarded after the referred client completes their service and payment.

Reward formula:

**150 fixed bonus points + 1 point for every 10,000 VND spent on the referred client's first invoice**

Example:

If the new client spends **1,000,000 VND**, the referring member receives:

* 150 fixed bonus points
* 100 invoice-based points

Total:

**250 reward points**

## 5. Returning Visits With Referred Friends

Lan Pink Tattoo appreciates members who continue supporting their friends and encouraging them to return.

After a referred client completes their first visit, future visits made independently do not automatically generate additional rewards for the original referrer.

However, if the member actively invites, accompanies, assists with booking, or directly reconnects the referred client with the studio, additional referral rewards may be granted.

Current formula for returning visits with a member:

**100 fixed bonus points + 1 point for every 20,000 VND spent on the returning client's invoice**

Example:

Client B was originally referred by Member A.

Later, Member A encourages Client B to return for another service worth **1,000,000 VND**.

Member A receives:

* 100 fixed bonus points
* 50 invoice-based points

Total:

**150 reward points**

Examples:

| Returning Client Invoice | Fixed Bonus | Invoice Points | Total Reward Points |
| -----------------------: | ----------: | -------------: | ------------------: |
|              500,000 VND |         100 |             25 |                 125 |
|            1,000,000 VND |         100 |             50 |                 150 |
|            2,000,000 VND |         100 |            100 |                 200 |
|            3,000,000 VND |         100 |            150 |                 250 |
|            5,000,000 VND |         100 |            250 |                 350 |

These rewards apply only when there is a clear referral, invitation, booking assistance, or direct involvement from the member before payment is completed.

This structure allows members to be rewarded not only for bringing in new clients but also for continuing to share positive experiences and helping friends return.

---

## 6. What Can Reward Points Be Used For?

Referral rewards are added to the member's available point balance.

Members may redeem points for vouchers that can be used at Lan Pink Tattoo.

Current redemption rates:

| Points Required | Voucher Value |
| --------------: | ------------: |
|      500 points |    50,000 VND |
|      950 points |   100,000 VND |
|    1,800 points |   200,000 VND |
|    4,400 points |   500,000 VND |

Vouchers are valid only at Lan Pink Tattoo and cannot be exchanged for cash.

---

## 7. Program Conditions

To receive referral rewards, the following conditions must be met:

* The referred client must provide the referrer's information when booking or before payment.
* Rewards are granted only after the referred client completes payment.
* Each new client can be associated with only one referrer.
* Self-referrals are not allowed.
* Duplicate, invalid, or fraudulent referrals are not eligible.
* Lan Pink Tattoo reserves the right to verify referral information and reject rewards when details are unclear.
* Reward rates may be adjusted over time according to studio policies.

---

## 8. How to Identify a Referrer

When booking or visiting the studio, the referred client simply needs to say:

> I was referred to Lan Pink Tattoo by [Referrer's Name].

After the service is completed, Lan Pink Tattoo will verify the information and award points if all requirements are met.
