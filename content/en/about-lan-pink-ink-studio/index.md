# Lan Pink Tattoo – Private Tattoo Studio in Ho Chi Minh City

![Female Tattoo Artist Lan Pink](https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/cuongpham0212/lanpinktattoo-image@main/images/tho-xam-nu-lanpink.webp)
