## How to estimate a tattoo price before asking

You can roughly estimate a tattoo price by checking three things: size, detail level, and whether the design needs custom work. The more time the design needs, the more the price may change.

### Why centimeters are not enough for pricing

A simple 5cm line tattoo can take much less time than a 5cm design with a face, fur, scales, patterns, or dense shading. Size matters, but detail changes everything.

## When should you simplify the design?

If your budget is fixed, the artist can help simplify details while keeping the main feeling of the design. This is often better than forcing a complex tattoo into a low price.

## Should you choose the cheapest tattoo price?

The cheapest price is not always the best choice. For meaningful, detailed, or long-lasting tattoos, it is often better to choose a design that fits your body and can heal well.

### When should you increase your budget?

- detailed small tattoos
- pet portrait tattoos
- shading-heavy designs
- cover ups or tattoo restoration
- large tattoos in difficult placements
