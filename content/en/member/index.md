## Why Join The Lan Pink Membership Program?

The Lan Pink Membership Program is designed for returning clients who want to receive long-term benefits, exclusive promotions, and priority support for future tattoo projects.

### Benefits of becoming a member

- earn points after every tattoo session
- receive birthday rewards and seasonal promotions
- enjoy referral bonuses when introducing friends
- access member-only campaigns and discounts
- receive priority consultation support

## How Does The Membership System Work?

Joining the membership program is free. Members collect points based on completed tattoo services and can redeem rewards according to the current program rules.

### Membership process

1. register as a Lan Pink member
2. receive points after each completed service
3. accumulate points for higher membership benefits
4. redeem available rewards and promotions
