Thank you for your interest in Lan Pink Tattoo.

To ensure the best experience for every client and help the consultation, design, and tattoo process run smoothly, please take a moment to review the policies below before using our services.

## 1. Booking & Studio Visit Policy

Lan Pink Tattoo is a private tattoo studio operating primarily by appointment to provide a comfortable, focused, and private experience for each client.

### Walk-in Clients

Walk-in visitors are welcome during our regular working hours:

- Tuesday to Sunday
- 1:00 PM - 10:00 PM

If Lan is currently tattooing another client, please allow a short waiting time so that each session can receive full attention and care.

### Appointments

Clients are encouraged to book in advance through Facebook Messenger or social media channels to:

- Receive detailed consultation.
- Have custom designs prepared in advance.
- Minimize waiting time.
- Secure a preferred time slot.

### Outside Regular Hours

For appointments scheduled outside normal working hours, a small booking deposit may be requested depending on the situation.

Any deposit paid will be deducted from the final service cost upon completion.

### Rescheduling or Cancellation

If you need to reschedule or cancel your appointment, please notify the studio at least 24 hours in advance whenever possible.

## 2. Tattoo Touch-Up Policy

Lan Pink Tattoo strives to ensure every tattoo heals properly and maintains the best possible quality over time.

### Free Touch-Up

Clients are eligible for one free touch-up within 30 days of the tattoo session if noticeable fading occurs due to natural healing or individual skin characteristics.

### Cases Not Covered

The free touch-up policy does not apply when:

- Aftercare instructions are not followed.
- Scabs or healing skin are picked or removed.
- The tattoo is exposed to swimming pools, beaches, long water immersion, steam rooms, or unsuitable environments during healing.
- Unrecommended aftercare products are used.
- Physical damage affects the tattooed area.

### Excluded Placement Areas

Due to naturally higher friction and skin turnover, the following areas are not covered by the touch-up policy:

- Fingers.
- Palms.
- Soles of the feet.
- Other high-friction areas.

## 3. Tattoo Aftercare Guidelines

To help your tattoo heal properly and maintain its appearance, please:

- Keep the tattoo clean and dry.
- Avoid scratching or peeling scabs.
- Limit direct sun exposure during the healing period.
- Avoid swimming, soaking, or steam rooms for at least 14 days.
- Follow all aftercare instructions provided by Lan Pink Tattoo.

## 4. Service Eligibility

Lan Pink Tattoo reserves the right to refuse service in the following situations:

- Clients under 18 years of age.
- Pregnant or breastfeeding individuals.
- Clients who do not provide accurate information regarding their health conditions.
- Individuals with medical conditions that may affect tattooing or healing.
- Individuals under the influence of alcohol or other substances before the appointment.
- Any situation deemed unsuitable for tattooing at the time of consultation.

## 5. Tattoo Content Policy

Lan Pink Tattoo reserves the right to refuse tattoo requests that:

- Violate Vietnamese law.
- Promote hatred, discrimination, or violence.
- Infringe upon intellectual property rights or personal privacy.
- Conflict with the studio's professional standards and values.
