## Why Tattoo Prices Vary So Much in Ho Chi Minh City

Tattoo prices are not only based on the size of the tattoo.

A tattoo quote may depend on:

- Tattoo size
- Tattoo placement
- Linework detail
- Shading or color
- Skin area and body curve
- Custom design time
- Number of sessions
- Artist experience
- Studio location
- Whether the studio is in a tourist area

This is why two tattoos that look similar in size may have very different prices.

## Mini Tattoo Cost

Mini tattoos are usually more affordable because they are smaller and require less working time.

They are popular with travelers, first-time clients and people who want something personal but simple.

Common mini tattoo ideas include:

- Small symbols
- Names or initials
- Minimal flowers
- Tiny animals
- Couple tattoos
- Zodiac signs
- Personal meaningful marks

## Fine Line Tattoo Cost

Fine line tattoos are delicate and elegant, but they still require control, clean technique and careful placement.

The cost depends on how detailed the design is. A small fine line tattoo with simple lines may cost less, while a more detailed piece may require more time.

Fine line tattoos are especially popular among clients looking for subtle, personal and feminine tattoo designs.

## Anime Tattoo and Custom Tattoo Cost

Anime tattoos, character tattoos and custom illustration tattoos often cost more than simple line tattoos because they may include:

- Character accuracy
- Facial details
- Costume details
- Shading
- Color planning
- Larger placement areas
- Longer working time

For example, an anime tattoo on the upper arm may be quoted very differently from one studio to another.

Some international clients have told us they received quotes around 10 million VND for similar work in other places, while Lan Pink Tattoo may be able to offer a much more affordable quote depending on the final size, design and detail level.

## Large Tattoo Project Cost

Large tattoos such as half sleeves, full sleeves, back pieces or Japanese-inspired tattoos are usually priced by project or session.

The final cost depends on:

- Body coverage
- Background work
- Line complexity
- Shading
- Color
- Number of sessions
- Healing time between sessions

Large projects should always be discussed before booking so the artist can estimate the time and design direction properly.

## Tourist Area Prices vs Private Studio Prices

Tattoo studios in central tourist areas often have higher rent, higher advertising costs and more walk-in demand.
