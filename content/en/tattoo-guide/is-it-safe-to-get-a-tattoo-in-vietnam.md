## What Makes a Tattoo Studio Safe?

A safe tattoo studio should care about more than the final photo.

Important safety factors include:

- Clean tattoo setup
- Single-use needles
- Proper skin preparation
- Professional tattoo materials
- Clear consultation
- Careful placement discussion
- Aftercare instructions
- No rushing during the session

A good tattoo experience should feel calm, clear and professional from consultation to aftercare.

## Single-Use Needles Matter

One of the most important things to check is whether the studio uses single-use tattoo needles.

At Lan Pink Tattoo, disposable materials are not reused. Every client should have a clean and properly prepared setup.

This is a basic safety standard and should never be compromised.

## Avoid Choosing Only by the Lowest Price

A very low tattoo quote can be tempting, especially while traveling.

But price should not be the only factor.

Before booking, you should also consider:

- Does the artist show real previous work?
- Is the studio clean?
- Does the artist explain the process clearly?
- Are needles single-use?
- Is aftercare explained?
- Do you know who will tattoo you?
- Is the design custom or copied quickly?

A safe tattoo is not just about saving money. It is about trusting the artist and the process.

## Tattoo Aftercare Is Part of Safety

A tattoo does not end when the session is finished.

Good aftercare helps protect the tattoo while the skin heals.

Basic aftercare usually includes:

- Keeping the tattoo clean
- Avoiding scratching or picking
- Avoiding swimming during early healing
- Avoiding strong sun exposure
- Following the artist's aftercare instructions

If you are traveling after your tattoo, tell the artist your travel schedule so they can give suitable advice.
