Misa Cat Portrait Tattoo – A Permanent Memory of a Special Companion

Some tattoos are more than artwork on skin.

They become a way to preserve memories, emotions, and the special companions who have been part of our lives.

This portrait tattoo of Misa is one of the meaningful custom projects created by Lan Pink – the female tattoo artist behind Lan Pink Tattoo (Lan Pink Ink) in Ho Chi Minh City.

It was also the client’s sixth tattoo with Lan Pink and the largest piece they had done together so far.

The Story Behind the Tattoo

For many animal lovers, a pet is not just a pet. They are family.

Misa was a beloved companion with a unique personality, expression, and presence. The goal of this tattoo was not simply to copy a reference photo, but to capture the details that made Misa truly special.

From the eyes and facial expression to the soft texture of the fur, every detail was carefully considered during the design process.

Instead of creating a direct copy of the photo, Lan Pink added her own artistic touch to give the piece more depth while still preserving Misa’s familiar expression and emotional presence.

Design and Tattoo Process

Pet Portrait Tattoo projects often require more time and attention than many standard tattoo designs.

To capture the character of a beloved pet, every element needs to be handled carefully:

  • Fur texture.
  • Smooth transitions between light and shadow.
  • Natural facial expression.
  • Depth and emotion in the eyes.

This tattoo was created using Fine Line Tattoo and Black & Grey Tattoo techniques to achieve a soft, detailed, and realistic result.

It is the kind of personalized work many clients look for when searching for a female tattoo artist in Ho Chi Minh City who understands custom pet tattoos and meaningful memorial pieces.

The Finished Tattoo

After hours of detailed work, Misa’s portrait was brought to life with the specific features and expression the client wanted to remember.

At Lan Pink Tattoo, projects like this always carry a special meaning. They are not only tattoos, but long-lasting memories that stay with the client for years to come.

Client Review

After the tattoo was completed, the client shared a very positive review about their experience at the studio.

They described Lan Pink Tattoo’s newly opened studio in District 12 as professional, creative, warm, and welcoming. From the first design stage to the final detailed result, the process was handled with care, dedication, and precision.

This was the client’s sixth tattoo with Lan Pink and also their largest one so far. What made the piece especially meaningful was how accurately Lan Pink captured Misa’s details and expression while adding her own artistic touch.
