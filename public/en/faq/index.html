Does getting a tattoo hurt?

Pain level depends on placement and your personal tolerance. Bony areas like ribs, wrists, and ankles hurt more than fleshy areas like biceps or thighs. At Lan Pink Tattoo, we use high-quality needles and proper technique to minimize pain. Most clients describe it as “tolerable discomfort”.

What should I avoid eating after a tattoo?

Avoid for the first 2 weeks to ensure proper healing and vibrant color:

  1. Beef, water spinach: Cause dark scarring/keloids
  2. Seafood: May cause allergic reactions and itching
  3. Sticky rice, chicken: Can cause inflammation/pus
  4. Alcohol, stimulants: Thins blood and fades ink

Drink lots of water, eat pork and fruits to help healing.

How do I take care of a new tattoo?

First 3 days: Wash with saline, pat dry, apply thin layer of Bepanthen. Wrap with plastic when going out.
Days 4-14: No more wrapping. Apply cream 3-4 times/day. Let scabs fall off naturally, do not pick.
After 2 weeks: Use moisturizer, apply SPF 50+ sunscreen when outside to preserve color.

How long before I can get a touch-up?

At least 3-4 weeks after your first session, once skin is fully healed. Lan Pink Tattoo offers one free touch-up within 2 months if needed due to technical issues.

Can tattoos be removed?

Yes, via laser removal. However, removal is more painful than tattooing, costs 5-10x more, and takes 5-10 sessions. Think carefully before getting inked. We also offer cover-ups for old/poor quality tattoos.
